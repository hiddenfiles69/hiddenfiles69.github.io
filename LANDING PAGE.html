Hidden Files is a dynamic online forum dedicated to fostering open, intelligent, and inclusive discussions around technology. Our mission create a safe space where curious minds and experts alike can share knowledge, explore hidden truths, and challenge mainstream narratives.

Our goal is to build a trusted community that empowers individuals through transparency, education, and the collective pursuit of digital awareness and security.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can users post anonymously?

Yes, we allow anonymous or guest posting, but it's often restricted to reduce spam. Some platforms let users create "throwaway" accounts with minimal info for privacy.

Can users report posts or other users?

Yes, we include a "Report" button on posts. Reported content is reviewed by moderators, and action can be taken depending on forum rules.

Community Guidelines

Welcome to Hidden FILE — a space where thoughts are free, honest, and respected. To keep this a safe and supportive place for everyone, we ask all users to follow these community guidelines:

1. Respect Others

  • No hate speech, harassment, bullying, or personal attacks.
  • We’re here to share and reflect — not to harm or shame.
  • Respect differences in opinion and experience.

2. Keep It Safe

  • Do not post anything that promotes violence, self-harm, or illegal activities.
  • Avoid sharing personal information (yours or others') like addresses, phone numbers, or private messages.

3. Stay on Topic

  • Hidden FILE is a place for meaningful thoughts and reflections.
  • Please avoid spam, unrelated promotions, or repetitive posts.

4. Be Honest, But Kind

  • This is a space for real thoughts. Be truthful — but mindful of how your words may affect others.
  • Share your thoughts, not hate.

5. No NSFW or Inappropriate Content

  • Keep the content safe for general audiences.
  • Avoid explicit, graphic, or suggestive material.

6. Report What Doesn’t Belong

  • If you see a post that violates these guidelines, help keep the community safe by reporting it.
